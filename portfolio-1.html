Project

Beyond Dark is the graduation project of Mortal Mind Games, the team developing the title, 4 months for development the project will be launched in the LevelUp Event.

Game

Beyond Dark is a horror-action game where the key feature will be a transport between 2 different dimensions, In the first dimension, The Mortal Realm, the player will be trapped withing a village spcaping a horrible entity that pursuits the player, In the second dimension, Vorgon's Realm, the player will batlle the entity's minions, and the entity (Vorgon) itself to free the village. Find out more in the future!
