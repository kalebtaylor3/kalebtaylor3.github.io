Project

Children Of The Crystal (COTC) is a rapid development project developed during my second year of Schooling. Our small team was given the task of creating a side-scroller within a limited time frame. As the project lead, I took charge of overseeing the development process, managing the team, and personally completing all programming tasks. This experience has provided valuable insights into project management and team coordination, as well as expanding my knowledge of C# and programming concepts. Despite encountering various challenges, we were able to overcome them and deliver a successful project.

Game

Embark on an exhilarating co-op adventure with Anselm and Aurora as they explore an ancient temple and stumble upon mysterious crystals that transport them to other dimensions. Trapped within the temple, they must use their newfound dimensional powers to navigate challenging puzzles and uncover the secrets of the temple's past. With stunning visuals and immersive gameplay, this thrilling puzzle-solving adventure offers an experience unlike any other.

My Work

As the team lead for Children Of The Crystal, I took on numerous challenges that demanded an extensive range of skills. As the sole programmer, I had to ensure that every aspect of the game's development was executed with precision and accuracy. This required a deep understanding of programming concepts and advanced techniques, such as writing shaders, that were essential for achieving the game's stunning visuals. Additionally, I faced the challenge of managing a team and coordinating their efforts to meet strict deadlines. Through perseverance and dedication, I was able to overcome these obstacles and deliver a highly polished game that received positive feedback from players and industry professionals alike.
