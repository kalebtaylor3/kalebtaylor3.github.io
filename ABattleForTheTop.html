Project

A Battle for the Top is a passion project my friends and I started working on after graduating from a game development program, with the goal of sharpening our skills and expanding our portfolios for the games industry. ABFTT is still very much in early development. Fresh off winning awards for our work on Beyond Dark, we poured our creativity and dedication into crafting this ambitious 3D action platformer, blending fast-paced gameplay with strategic card mechanics. It is a testament to our drive and talent as we embark on our professional journeys!

Game

A Battle for the Top is a fast-paced 3D action platformer with card-based mechanics. Players navigate five themed zones—Fantasy, Western, Prehistoric, Horror, and a Final Zone, battling bosses and overcoming platforming challenges. Cards serve as tools for movement and combat, with upgrades and strategic deck management key to progression. Defeat bosses to earn rare cards and unlock powerful upgrades, but beware—three strikes reset the game. With shortcuts, hazards, and dynamic challenges, every decision counts in the climb to the top!

My Work

As the sole programmer for A Battle for the Top, I implemented all gameplay systems, including the core platforming mechanics, card-based movement and combat, and dynamic boss AI with unique hazards for each zone. I developed and refined systems for deck management, card upgrades, and progression, ensuring smooth integration with platforming and combat. Additionally, I programmed features like shortcuts, environmental challenges, and multi-platform controls, while optimizing performance and balancing gameplay to provide a challenging yet rewarding experience. From zone-specific mechanics to UI and save systems, I was responsible for bringing every technical aspect of the game to life.
