Project

Mech Mayhem was developed through the collective efforts of a four-person development team, including myself and three other developers, during the MechJam IV week long game jam challenge. Using the Unity engine and C# programming language, our team invested five days in crafting an immersive gaming experience. MechJam IV's theme inspired us to plunge players into the heart of mechanized warfare. Despite the time constraints, our team successfully implemented a variety of gameplay mechanics, featuring intense mech battles, fun gunplay, and AI. The result is an exhilarating gaming experience that captures the essence of MechJam IV. This collaborative project served as a crucible for learning valuable skills in game development, teamwork, and efficient time management. Creating a fully functional game within the confines of the game jam timeframe not only met the challenge head-on but also provided an invaluable opportunity for skill enhancement and collaborative growth.

Game

You are mech pilot who has been ordered to defend his capital ship from an all out assault. Your mech contains an arsenal of weapons and abilities that you will be able to switch to on the fly. Your mech is capable of ground and aerial combat to fight various types of enemies. How long will you be able to push back the assault?
