Project

Mech Mayhem was developed through the collective efforts of a four-person development team, including myself and three other developers, during the MechJam IV week long game jam challenge. Using the Unity engine and C# programming language, our team invested five days in crafting an immersive gaming experience. MechJam IV's theme inspired us to plunge players into the heart of mechanized warfare. Despite the time constraints, our team successfully implemented a variety of gameplay mechanics, featuring intense mech battles, fun gunplay, and AI. The result is an exhilarating gaming experience that captures the essence of MechJam IV. This collaborative project served as a crucible for learning valuable skills in game development, teamwork, and efficient time management. Creating a fully functional game within the confines of the game jam timeframe not only met the challenge head-on but also provided an invaluable opportunity for skill enhancement and collaborative growth. (***Mobile port coming soon!***)

Game

You are mech pilot who has been ordered to defend his capital ship from an all out assault. Your mech contains an arsenal of weapons and abilities that you will be able to switch to on the fly. Your mech is capable of ground and aerial combat to fight various types of enemies. How long will you be able to push back the assault?

My Work

In my role as lead developer of the Mech Mayhem development team, I took on the responsibility of programming critical elements that laid the foundation for the game's immersive experience. As the primary programmer, I spearheaded the development of integral gameplay systems, including mech abilities, weapon switching mechanics, and enemy AI interactions, all within the Unity engine using the C# programming language. My contributions ensured the seamless integration of dynamic features, such as ground and aerial combat, enhancing the player's engagement in the face of an all-out assault on their capital ship. Collaborating closely with the team, I demonstrated effective time management, ensuring the efficient development of these systems while staying within the game's scope. Throughout this project, I further refined my expertise in Unity development and deepened my proficiency in C# programming, underscoring my commitment to delivering a polished and fully functional game within the demanding timeframe of MechJam IV. This experience has not only fortified my technical skills but has also enriched my collaborative capabilities and time management efficiency.
