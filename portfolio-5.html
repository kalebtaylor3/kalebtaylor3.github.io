Project

Mech Mayhem was a five-day team project created for MechJam IV. It is a good example of collaborative rapid delivery, where I helped turn a broad concept into a working product by focusing on the systems that mattered most and integrating them quickly with the rest of the team's work.

Technical Focus

The project centered on input handling, ability switching, combat behaviour, and enemy interactions under a short production schedule. The challenge was not just building each feature independently, but making sure they worked together cleanly enough to support a complete playable loop in under a week.

My Work

As lead developer and primary programmer, I implemented the core gameplay systems in Unity using C#, including mech abilities, weapon switching, and enemy AI interactions. I also worked closely with the rest of the team to keep the feature set realistic and integrated. This project reflects my ability to move quickly, collaborate well, and still ship coherent systems when time is limited.
