Project

Evermania is a mobile game developed solely by my self and Roy Thomblison, with the goal of learning about mobile development and generating ad revenue. The game was created using Unity and programmed with C#. One of the key features of the game is the infinite grid system, which allows for a unique and never-ending gameplay experience. The game also includes a cosmetic item shop and playable ads. Through this project, I learned a lot about mobile development and expanded my knowledge with Unity.

Game

Evermania is a fast-paced and addictive infinite vertical scroller that will keep you entertained for hours. The game is set in a colorful and vibrant world where the player must jump and dodge obstacles to progress as far as possible. The game is easy to pick up but difficult to master, making it a perfect time-killer for anyone looking for a fun and challenging experience on their mobile device.

My Work

As the lead programmer of Evermania, I worked on every aspect of the game, from the infinite grid system and movement controls to the cosmetic item shop and playable ads. I also focused on making the game fun and addicting for players. Throughout the development process, I learned a lot about mobile development and Unity, and how to create an engaging and entertaining game. Overall, I am proud of what I was able to accomplish with Evermania and am excited to release it to the mobile app stores.
